➡️SAWAL[1]:

Agar Ayat-e-Sajdah sunne ki niyyat na thi,
lekin bilakasur sun liya to sajda karna kya hai?
A) mustahab hai
B) wajib hai
C) sunnat nahi
D) llm hasil karenge
⭐JAWAB:
Agar Ayat-e-Sajdah sunne ki niyyat na thi,
Sunne wale ke liye zaroori nahin hai bilakasur suni ho,
bilakasur sunne se bhi Sajdah wajib ho jata hai.
